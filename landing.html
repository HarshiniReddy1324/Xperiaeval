Methodology

Explainable scoring, not a black box

Every score breaks down by dimension so reviewers can see why a candidate landed where they did — and defend the decision in an audit.

Overall = Resume×25% + Answers×25% + Evidence×30% + Communication×20%

Green — recommend interview ≥ 80
Amber — review recommended 60 – 79
Red — deprioritize < 60
